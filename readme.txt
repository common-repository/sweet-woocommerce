== Description ==

Enhanced Ecommerce Sweet Analytics Plugin for WooCommerce is a free plugin for WooCommerce stores which allows businesses to quickly
set up the Sweet Analytics Tracker, including the Enhanced Ecommerce feature. You can track user behavior, monitor conversions
and fully understand which channels are driving those conversions. This will allow you to be able to to take informed decisions to
optimize your marketing and drive business growth.

There are hundreds of marketing tools out there, but none of them allow you to push all your marketing data into a single place. Are
you often left wondering if 3rd party platforms such as Adwords and Facebook are attributing the same conversions to themselves and
numbers don't seem to add up? Sweet Analytics is here to bridge the gap between your marketing platforms and reality to provide you 
with accurate information that you can use confidently in your day to day activities.


== Features ==

1. Dead simple installation. A few clicks are all that's needed for you to get full access to Sweet Analytics Tracker insights.
1. Adds incredible new reports within Sweet Analytics including: Tracker Insights, Attribution Compare Tool, Tracker Summary, and much more!
1. Allows you to tie user behaviour with your customer data. Know exactly where users came from before making a purchase!
1. Custom Event Tracking

== Installation ==

*   Install and Activate the plugin in Wordpress.
*   Navigate to the plugin page via Settings -> Sweet Analytics.
*   Select the "Settings" button to show the available settings that one can configure.
*   Simply copy and paste the Tracker ID from your Sweet Analytics dashboard in the space provided.
*   Check the "Enable Tracker" option for the plugin to automatically install the tracking code on all pages of your site, then click "Save".
*   To stop tracking, simply unselect the "Enable Tracker" option and click "Save". This will stop all tracking from happening on your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Sweet Analytics =

Sweet Analytics is an analytics and automation platform that is built to give you a unique view into your marketing data through insights and action. Visit our website to learn more: [https://sweetanalytics.com](https://sweetanalytics.com).

= Do I have to pay? =

You do not have to pay anything to install this plugin. However you do need to be signed up with [Sweet Analytics](https://sweetanalytics.com) to access all of the reporting and to get your unique Tracking ID.

= Where can I find my Tracker ID =

Log in to Sweet Analytics and navigate to `Settings -> Integrations -> Sweet Tracker` and you will see your Tracking ID. Simply copy and paste this into your Woocommerce plugin and enable tracking to start pushing data into Sweet Analytics.

= Is this plugin GDPR Compliant? =

Yes, our plugin is under GDPR compliance. For more information, read the [privacy policy](https://sweetanalytics.com/privacy-policy/) of our plugin.
